Our Simple Tips

Plan Meals Wisely

Tip 1: Create a weekly meal plan and shopping list to buy only what you need, reducing the chance of unused food items spoiling.

Proper Storage

Tip 2: Store perishable items like fruits, vegetables, and dairy products in the correct conditions to prolong their freshness and prevent them from spoiling prematurely.

Use Every Bit

Tip 3: Get creative with leftovers by incorporating them into new dishes or freezing them for future meals instead of letting them go to waste.

Understand Expiry Dates

Tip 4: Understand the difference between "sell by," "use by," and "best before" dates to prevent throwing away perfectly good food unnecessarily.

Compost

Tip 5: Utilize food scraps for composting to divert organic waste from landfills and create nutrient-rich soil for gardening.
